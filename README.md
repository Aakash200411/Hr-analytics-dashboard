# 👥 HR Analytics Dashboard (Power BI)

## Overview

This project focuses on workforce analytics using Power BI. The dashboard provides insights into employee attrition, workforce demographics, departmental performance, and HR KPIs to support data-driven decision-making.

---

## Objectives

- Monitor HR KPIs
- Analyze employee attrition
- Understand workforce distribution
- Compare department performance
- Support HR decision-making

---

## Tech Stack

- Power BI
- DAX
- Power Query

---

## Dataset

HR Analytics Dataset

Includes:

- Employee Information
- Department
- Job Role
- Education
- Income
- Years at Company
- Attrition
- Job Satisfaction

## Dashboard Features

### KPI Cards

- Total Employees
- Attrition Count
- Attrition Rate
- Average Age
- Average Salary
- Average Years at Company

### Workforce Analysis

- Employees by Department
- Employees by Job Role
- Gender Distribution
- Education Analysis
- Age Distribution

### Attrition Analysis

- Attrition by Department
- Attrition by Age Group
- Attrition by Job Role
- Attrition by Salary
- Attrition by Years at Company

### Interactive Filters

- Department
- Education
- Gender
- Job Role
- Age Group
